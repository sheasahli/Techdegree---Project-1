A little about me..

I’m an aspiring web designer with career experience in the fashion industry and a passion for tech. During this unprecedented time, I saw the opportunity of time to expand my skillset and take a leap into the tech industry.

My career in fashion began in 2016 with a jewelry company whose designs incorporate the southern California lifestyle. I quickly progressed through their wholesale channel and directly affected the brand’s growth to a global retailer with over 16 store locations and major partnerships with the nation’s top retailers.
